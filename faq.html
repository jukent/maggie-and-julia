Our venue is at an altitude of 5,371 feet. If you're coming from sea level, the things to keep in mind are:

  • 1. Sunscreen is a must! The sun's rays are stronger up here and you can burn faster.
  • 2. Drink LOTS of water! To ward off headaches, drink an extra liter or two.
  • 3. Watch your alcohol. We want you to party and have a good time, but please be mindful that you will get drunk much faster in Colorado.
